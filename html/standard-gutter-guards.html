Gutter Guards

Next generation gutter guards

Standard Gutter Guard is a fourth generation product designed and manufactured in Washington State. Through years of development, testing, and proving, this design is one of the most easily installed, functional, and affordable stainless steel gutter guards on the market today. It will work with existing gutters as easily as new gutters.

  • No clog no overflow 5 year warranty
  • Stainless steel mesh (.0213” opening) to keep out even the smallest debris • Simple maintenance for unrestricted water flow
  • Weather and pest resistant
  • Simple installation
  • Fits most standard gutter installations
  • Slightly recessed to prevent overflows during saturation
  • Strengthens the gutter and stays securely in place
  • Virtually invisible from the ground
  • Available in white or brown

What makes Standard Gutter Guards different?

Material

The base of Standard Gutter Guard is comprised of .019” aluminum supplied from Alsco Metals Corporation and is made of 92.08% recycled aluminum. It is made from the same material as downspouts and has the same manufacturer material warranty.

The stainless steel screen is purchased from McNichols Company in Washington State. The steel mesh is comprised of a 304 alloy, square weave, 30 mesh, .012” wire, and with .0213” openings. Essentially, this screen was specifically chosen because the holes are small enough to keep out roof granules, pine needles, and any larger debris from entering the gutters. However, the holes are large enough to allow pollen and bio-organic material to pass through and flush out the gutter system.

Construction

Standard Gutter Guard is produced using the roll forming method. Roll forming is ideal for producing constant profile parts with long lengths and a consistent outcome. The stainless steel screen is held in place to the solid aluminum substrate using pittsburg seams. There is no glue, pins, or caulking used in the production of Standard Gutter
