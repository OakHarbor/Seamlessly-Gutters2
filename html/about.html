About Us

Who Is Matt?

I started this business in 2017. I wanted to put my skills to good use and to offer quality work for Island County and surrounding areas at an affordable price. I feel I can do this with my 15 plus years of experience. I learned my skills working for a sheet metal roofing company in Ogden Utah, where I traveled all up the West Coast doing installs.

My goal for the company is to continue to offer quality service at an affordable price, using top of the line products. We offer a 5 year limited warranty on installations and gutters are backed with a 20 year manufactured warranty. We want to expand our company to add full time employees with another gutter truck. We will expand our company to install metal roofs and under deck drainage systems.

Our mission is to give every customer the attention to detail their homes deserve and guarantee our work to last.
